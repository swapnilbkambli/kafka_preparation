  - Support the deployment, configuration, and maintenance of Kafka platform.
  - Troubleshoot incidents, perform root cause analysis and implement reliability improvements.

TO BE SUCCESSFUL, YOU WILL NEED

  - Overall 10+ years of experience and preferably 7-10 years of experience working on Kafka.
  - Familiarity with Kafka concepts and operations.
  - Understanding of containerization and orchestration.
  - Experience in Kubernetes is preferred.
  - Strong analytical skills and a proactive approach to identifying and resolving issues.
  - Familiarity with infrastructure as code. Proficiency in observability and logging tools.
  - Experience with Unix administration skills.
  - Ability to work effectively within a team. Weekend support required.
